About Me

With a passion for user-centered design, I specialize in crafting digital products that are not only visually appealing but also highly functional and accessible. My journey in design began with a curiosity for how people interact with technology, leading me to explore various facets of UI/UX, from research and wireframing to prototyping and usability testing.

I thrive on collaboration and am always eager to learn new approaches and technologies that can enhance the design process. My goal is to create meaningful experiences that resonate with users and drive positive impact.

When I'm not designing, you can find me exploring new art exhibitions, hiking nature trails, or diving into a good book.
